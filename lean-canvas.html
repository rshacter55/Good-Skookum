A

Customer Segments

 Identifies the specific market segment or audience who will most benefit from your product or service using the Jobs To Be Done framework.

  • Retired Professionals

    Persona Overview

    Linda, aged 65-75, former healthcare professional from the suburbs of Chicago, recently retired and experiencing a sense of isolation.

    Customer Jobs
    • Looking to remain mentally and socially active post-retirement,
    • Seeking to utilize her skillset in a meaningful way,
    • Desiring to connect with individuals who share similar professional interests
    Customer Pains
    • Struggle with the sudden lack of purpose post-retirement,
    • Difficulty in finding communities that cater to her age and professional interests,
    • Adapting to significant lifestyle changes

  • Empty Nesters

    Persona Overview

    Kevin, aged 50-60, an engineer from Austin, Texas, whose children have recently left home for college, leading to a noticeably emptier house and a desire to fill the void.

    Customer Jobs
    • Seeking engagement in activities that help fill the time previously spent on parenting,
    • Looking for new social circles beyond parenting networks,
    • Wishing to foster a sense of community with peers facing similar life changes
    Customer Pains
    • Feeling disconnected from long-standing social circles,
    • Coping with the transition to an empty nest,
    • Lacking opportunities to meet new people in a similar life stage

  • Widowed Individuals

    Persona Overview

    Maria, aged 70-80, widow living in a small town in Florida, dealing with the loss of a spouse and the subsequent emotional void.

    Customer Jobs
    • Seeking companionship and emotional support post-loss,
    • Desires to build a support network that understands her grief,
    • Wants to participate in activities that can bring joy and new purpose in life
    Customer Pains
    • Experiencing intense loneliness and potential depression after a spouse's death,
    • Difficulty in finding relatable support groups,
    • Overwhelmed by the need to rebuild social structures

    • Late Career Shifters

      Persona Overview

      Bob, aged 55-65, recently decided to leave his corporate job in Seattle to pursue his passion for photography, now looking for community support.

      Customer Jobs
      • Wants to connect with people who have undergone a similar career shift,
      • Interested in learning new skills and disciplines necessary for his new venture,
      • Desires mentorship and emotional support during this life transition
      Customer Pains
      • Facing the uncertainty and risks associated with changing careers later in life,
      • Difficulties in forming new networks outside the corporate world,
      • Lack of understanding from friends and family about his career decision

  • Digital Nomads

    Persona Overview

    Sophie, aged 50-60, freelance graphic designer who travels frequently, often feeling disconnected due to her nomadic lifestyle.

    Customer Jobs
    • Seeking a stable and understanding community that accommodates her lifestyle,
    • Desires consistent social interaction and companionship,
    • Wanting to share experiences with like-minded individuals on the move
    Customer Pains
    • Struggles with creating lasting friendships due to frequent travel,
    • Experiences social disconnection in new locations,
    • Faces challenges in maintaining a sense of community

    D

    Solution

    Presents your product or service that addresses the identified problem.

    Features

    • Interactive community platform tailor-made for people aged 50+
    • In-person and online networking events to form meaningful connections
    • Training programs in generative listening and leadership development
    • Volunteering opportunities to foster engagement within the community
    • Ongoing research and feedback mechanisms to tailor effective interventions

    Potential Ways-to-Play

    1. Mobile Application for Easy Access
    • Developing a mobile app specifically tailored to seniors and technologically less adept users to facilitate easier access and communication.
    1. Combining Offline and Online Elements
    • Hybrid approach by combining online platform services with real-world interaction opportunities, such as local meet-ups and community events.
    1. Intergenerational Programs
    • Facilitating connections between different age groups to share skills, life experiences, and foster mentorship relationships.
    1. Sophisticated Matching Algorithms
    • Utilizing data to match members with similar interests, challenges, and needs to create more meaningful and supportive relationships.
    1. Partnerships with Health and Wellness Organizations
    • Collaborating with organizations that offer mental health support, fitness programs, and wellness education to provide a holistic approach to combating loneliness.
    1. Educational Workshops and Training
    • Offering workshops on generative listening, leadership, technology use, and other areas that can empower members and give them the tools to build and maintain supportive communities.

    Aim for a solution that's 10x better than existing alternatives to make it worth the switch for your customers.
